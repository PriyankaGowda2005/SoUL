Event Guidelines

Complete guidelines for organizing events, protocols, and best practices for SoUL activities

🎨
Cultural Showcase Planning
Guidelines for organizing cultural exhibitions, art displays, and heritage showcases.
  • ✓ Curate diverse cultural representations and activities
  • ✓ Set up interactive cultural experience zones
  • ✓ Arrange traditional craft demonstrations
  • ✓ Coordinate cultural food stalls and vendors
  • ✓ Document cultural activities for future reference
🛠️
Skill Development Workshops
Guidelines for organizing hands-on workshops, training sessions, and skill-building activities.
  • ✓ Identify target audience and skill levels
  • ✓ Prepare workshop materials and tools in advance
  • ✓ Limit participant numbers for effective learning
  • ✓ Arrange practice sessions and feedback rounds
  • ✓ Provide take-home resources and follow-up support
💻
Technical Workshops
Specialized guidelines for technology, digital skills, and technical training workshops.
  • ✓ Ensure adequate computing equipment and internet connectivity
  • ✓ Prepare software installations and technical setup
  • ✓ Have technical support staff available
  • ✓ Create step-by-step tutorial materials
  • ✓ Plan troubleshooting procedures for common issues

⏰ Event Planning Timeline

8-12 Weeks Before
Initial Planning & Concept Development
Define event objectives, target audience, budget allocation, and form planning committee
6-8 Weeks Before
Venue & Vendor Booking
Secure venue, book catering, entertainment, and technical equipment
4-6 Weeks Before
Marketing & Promotion
Launch promotional campaign, create marketing materials, and open registration
2-4 Weeks Before
Logistics Coordination
Finalize volunteer assignments, confirm vendors, and prepare event materials
1 Week Before
Final Preparations
Conduct final briefings, confirm attendee count, and prepare contingency plans
Event Day
Event Execution
Setup, coordinate activities, manage volunteers, and ensure smooth operations
Post-Event
Wrap-up & Evaluation
Cleanup, thank stakeholders, document lessons learned, and evaluate success
