About SoUL Organization

SoUL is a voluntary student organization which was formed by a group of students in 2014 with the tagline: "Educating minds, Empowering lives".

Our Mission

  • Empower Young Talent :- To recognize and nurture the potential of young individuals in need.
  • Guide Them Towards Their Dreams :- To inspire confidence and provide direction, helping them stay on the right path to achieve their goals.
  • Enable Through Education :- To provide accessible and high-quality education, equipping them to thrive in today's competitive world.
  • Spread Joy and Create Impact :- To foster moments of happiness, bring smiles across generations, and build a community of hope and purpose.

Events We conduct

Highlights from our recent activities and community initiatives

SoUL Day

The SoUL volunteers organize SoUL Day every year which includes various events such as workshops, literary, cultural and sports. This day is celebrated as a remark of success where all the little kids learn many new things and enjoy a lot.

200+ Students January 2025

Festivals Celebrations

Every year we, volunteers of SoUL celebrate Deepavali and other festivals with the little hearts of Balmandir. It is ensured that the children aren't missing their families during these occasions. We make them feel special by distributing sweets.

50+ Families December 2024

Patriotic Festivities

Every Independence Day and Republic Day, Balmandir children proudly participate in the parade at the Hassan District Stadium, showcasing their respect for the nation. Their performances honor the sacrifices of our freedom fighters.

100+ Students November 2024

Alumni Meet

On SoUL Day, the active members organize the SoUL Alumni Meet, bringing together alumni, industry experts, and current members. The event fosters networking, mentorship, and knowledge sharing while also serving as a platform for alumni to provide valuable feedback and suggestions.

100+ Alumni December 2024
